Description

This is stockpile, a portfolio tracking web app geared to enable you to precisely track the performance of your stocks.

Stockpile will also has a few nifty features such as stock recommendations based on similar user picks, portfolio risk management tools, tax return calculators and much much more, stay for stockpile to come out of alpha development!

Stockpile is coded in flutter making the app accesabile via native ios and android as well as the web.
