Description

This is stockpile, a portfolio management tool that enables precise and automated analytics and monitoring of your investments across portfolios and asset classes.

Stockpile will feature automated trade imports, stock recommendations, portfolio risk management tools, tax return calculators and much much more, stay tuned for Stockpile to come out of alpha development!

Stockpile will target the web, android and iOS.
